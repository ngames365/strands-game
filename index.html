What is Strands Game?

Strands Game is a word puzzle game, similar to Connections, where players uncover themed words to fill a board. It is the latest addition to the popular Crossword games by The New York Times.

How to Play Strands Game?

  • Step 1: Look for words related to the puzzle's theme. Correctly identified theme words will turn blue when you use your finger to drag across or tap letters to select them.
  • Step 2: Utilize the hint button if you're stuck. You have up to 3 hints for each puzzle. Each hint will highlight the characters of one word, significantly aiding in its discovery.
  • Step 3: There are no non-theme words in this game, so focus solely on finding words that fit the puzzle's theme. Use hints strategically to reveal difficult words.

Additional Help: Since there are no non-theme words to find, the hints are your primary tool for assistance. Plan when to use them, especially for more challenging puzzles, to ensure you can uncover all theme-related words without running out of hints.
